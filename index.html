⚠️ Requirements
• Chrome / Edge / Opera on desktop or Android (Web BT not supported on iOS Safari or Firefox)
• Page must be served via https:// or localhost
• Quick local server: python3 -m http.server 8080 then open http://localhost:8080/26_NativeWebBT/

ℹ️ Services used
• Cooking Zone Service 0x8FF8 — characteristics 0x2C26–0x2C2C
• Cookware Service 0x8FF7 / 0x185D — Cookware Description 0x8FF6 / 0x2C25
